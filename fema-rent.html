What rent should I pay FEMA?

If you're a Maui fire survivor in FEMA Direct Housing, this tool calculates whether you qualify for the $50/month low-income rate, or the standard 25% of Fair Market Rent.

Tell us about your situation

Add up wages, unemployment, Social Security, disability — everyone in the household. Use post-disaster income (what you earn now), not what you earned before.
Built for Maui fire survivors as a free, private tool. No tracking, no signup, no data collected. Income limits sourced from HUD FY2025 Very Low Income (VLI) limits for Maui County. Maui County Fair Market Rents are HUD FY2026. Verify all amounts with your FEMA Recertification Advisor before relying on them. Calculations here are for orientation, not legal or financial advice. FEMA appeals are processed within 90 days; until decided you must continue paying full rent.
