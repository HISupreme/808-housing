Senior property tax exemption

If you own your home in Hawaiʻi and you're 60 or older, you probably qualify for a bigger property tax break than you're getting. This 3-minute walkthrough shows what you'd save and how to file.
Built for kupuna in Hawaiʻi as a free, private tool. No tracking, no signup, no data collected. Tax rates and exemption amounts reflect FY2025-2026 published by Honolulu, Maui, Hawaiʻi, and Kauaʻi counties. Numbers shown are estimates — actual savings depend on your assessed value and tier. Not legal or financial advice. For free help filing, call AARP Foundation Property Tax-Aide at 1-888-227-7669 or your county tax office. Filing deadlines: Honolulu and Kauaʻi Sept 30, Maui Dec 31, Hawaiʻi Dec 31 or June 30.
